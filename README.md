## 📋 About TFTT

**The Film Foundation Trust (TFFT)** is a Public Charitable Trust established on 7th September 2022.

- **Registered Office:** C-64, 3rd Floor, South Extension Part-II, New Delhi – 110049
- **Working Area:** Pan-India
- **Mode:** No Profit – No Loss

## 🎯 Our Mission

To provide meaningful, wholesome, healthy cinema to the Indian audience — prioritising the promotion of Indian culture, ethics, and national heritage.
